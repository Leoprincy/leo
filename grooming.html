Welcome to Leo Dog Grooming Center, where your pet's well-being and happiness are our top priorities. We understand that your furry friends are more than just pets; they are beloved members of your family. That's why we strive to provide the highest quality grooming services to keep them looking and feeling their best.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of grooming services to meet the unique needs of every dog. Our services include:

Bath & Brush: Our gentle and thorough bath and brush service ensures your pet's coat is clean, shiny, and free of tangles.

Haircut & Styling: Whether your dog needs a simple trim or a full makeover, our skilled groomers are here to help.

Nail Trimming: Regular nail trims are essential for your dog's comfort and health. We make the process quick and stress-free.

Ear Cleaning: Proper ear cleaning helps prevent infections and keeps your dog's ears healthy.

Teeth Brushing: Good dental hygiene is vital for your pet's overall health. Our teeth brushing service helps maintain a clean and healthy mouth.

Our Facility

Our state-of-the-art grooming facility is designed with your pet's comfort in mind. We use high-quality, pet-safe products and the latest grooming techniques to ensure the best results. Our grooming areas are clean, spacious, and equipped with the best tools to provide a safe and pleasant experience for your dog.

Why Choose Us?

Experienced Groomers: Our team of professional groomers is highly trained and experienced in handling dogs of all breeds and sizes.

Personalized Care: We understand that every dog is unique, and we tailor our services to meet the specific needs of each pet.

Stress-Free Environment: We prioritize creating a calm and relaxing environment to ensure a positive grooming experience.

Customer Satisfaction: Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has earned us a loyal clientele who trust us with their pets.

Our Services

Socialization Traiining

This method focuses on rewarding desired behaviors with treats, praise, or toys to encourage the dog to repeat those behaviors.

Agility Training

Involves teaching dogs to navigate obstacle courses. It enhances their physical fitness, mental stimulation, and bond with their handler.

Obidience Training

Focuses on teaching basic commands such as sit, stay, come, and heel. It establishes a foundation for good behavior and communication between the dog and owner.

Service Dog Training

Specialized training to perform tasks that assist individuals with disabilities, such as guide dogs for the blind or hearing dogs for the deaf.

Positive Reinforcement training

This method focuses on rewarding desired behaviors with treats, praise, or toys to encourage the dog to repeat those behaviors.
